Clue

A normal clue is a clue which can be resolved into a VALUE_NO or VALUE_YES relationship immediately. It will not be provide additional information in the future. It is distinct from a special clue.

  • e.g. Martin lives in the green house. (VALUE_YES)
  • e.g. Yohan does not drink tea. (VALUE_NO)

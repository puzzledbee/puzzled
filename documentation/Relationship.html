Object which represents a relationship between two items of different categories. By default, a relationship is defined as a VALUE_UNKNOWN; it can also take the VALUE_YES or VALUE_NO. A relationship can be visually represented on the puzzle grid by a GridCell, which is listening to mouse events so the user can manually enter constraints.
